

Toll Caculator is for caculating the toll fee.
The fee for car is 11kr, for motorbike is 8kr. 
During rush hour, it's 18kr for both car and motorbike.
The fee is charged everytime the veichle passed the road.
The maximum fee for a day is 60kr. 

You can make an array and put the time that the veichle passed. 
Use 24 hours system.
